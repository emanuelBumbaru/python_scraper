          ELISA Kits

          Invitrogen ELISA kits allow you to measure target-specific proteins with confidence.

          Cytokine, Chemokine, & Growth Factor ELISA Kits

          Our extensive portfolio covers a broad range of secreted, circulating proteins such as cytokines, chemokines, and growth factors for immunology and inflammation research. These proteins are important in cell development, proliferation, migration, differentiation, and maturation.

          Learn more

          Immuno-Oncology ELISA kits

          Immuno-Oncology Invitrogen ELISA kits allow detection of soluble immune checkpoint molecules and help to provide a comprehensive picture of cancer immunity. These assay kits are designed to deliver accurate protein quantitation in serum, plasma and cell culture supernatant samples.

          Learn more

          Neurobiology ELISA Kits

          Invitrogen offers high-quality ELISA kits for researchers studying neurological disorders or doing neuroscience-related research. These "neuroELISA" kits are designed for accurate, sensitive and fast AB (B-amyloid or amyloid beta), tau, and alpha-synuclein protein quantitation in a variety of sample types.

          Learn more

          Phospho-specific ELISA Kits

          Phosphorylation-specific Invitrogen ELISA kits are designed for researchers studying intracellular proteins involved in signaling pathways. These assay kits are designed to deliver accurate, sensitive, and fast protein quantitation of total and phosphorylated, modified, or cleavage site-specific proteins in cell lysates.

          Learn more

          Immunoglobulin ELISA Kits

          Immunoglobulin-specific and isotyping Invitrogen ELISA kits provide a fast, sensitive and convenient method to identify and quantify antibody classes, subclasses, and isotypes during and after hybridoma development to assess immune response.

          Learn more
