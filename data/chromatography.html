          Providing innovative chromatographic workflow solutions

          Put our latest innovations in ion, gas and liquid chromatography to work in your laboratory. We’ve focused on developing leading-edge workflow solutions—from sample preparation, chromatographic separation, seamless integration with mass spectrometry, and data management and analysis—to meet today’s ever increasing demands for analytical performance, productivity and ease of use.

          Chromatography categories
          Liquid Chromatography

          Redefining HPLC and UHPLC to give you more. Streamline your applications with our highly integrated solutions including systems, standard and bio-LC columns, autosamplers, detectors, fittings, software and more.

          LC-MS and LC-MS/MS

          Use our HPLC/UHPLC systems to connect with our mass spectrometry systems to confidently identify, deeply characterize and precisely quantify a vast range of compounds.

          Ion Chromatography

          Benefit from our 40 years experience. Whether you perform routine IC analyses or more demanding applications, choose the best solution for your application from our innovative portfolio of Thermo Scientific Dionex IC systems, columns, components, consumables, and software.

          IC-MS

          Employ IC-MS when you need to increase your IC detection capability, such as to detect and quantify unexpected co-elutions or confirm trace components.

          Gas Chromatography

          Delivering a new standard in routine GC analysis that increases productivity and reduces cost of ownership. Choose from advanced sampling handling solutions, modern GC systems, quality columns and state-of-the-art reporting, reagents and accessories.

          GC-MS and GC-MS/MS

          Solutions for the most challenging applications. Meet stringent requirements for performance, reliability, and value with our powerful GC-MS and GC-MS/MS instruments.

          Autosampler Vials and Closures

          You'll find the right vials and closures to match your application and autosampler requirements. Our innovative autosampler vials and closures and vial identification system offer improved sample security and integrity.

          Automated Sample Preparation

          Be more productive, reduce errors and get better results. These automated solutions perform high pressure solvent extraction of solid and semisolid samples, solid-phase extraction (SPE) from large-volume aqueous matrices to isolate trace organics, and solvent evaporation.

          Sample Preparation Consumables

          Offering solid phase extraction (SPE), solid supported liquid extraction (SLE), micro extraction by packed sorbent (MEPS), and manifolds and dispensers you can use in the process. Don’t overlook the syringe filters, protein precipitation and protein digest products.

          Chromatography Data Systems

          There’s a chromatography data system (CDS) software solution that’s right for your chromatography and mass spectrometry laboratory. We offer operating and data management solutions from single standalone systems to enterprise-wide solutions.
